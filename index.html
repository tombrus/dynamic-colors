    dynamic-colors

    JQuery extension to dynamically adjust colors on a page (demo below):

    Can be helpful while developing a proper color scheme (or schemes). You can also empower your users to tweak some colors to their liking.

    demo

    • adjust the colors with the color pickers
    • the first two colors are the background gradient, the other three are for the flag
    • copy the signature string for later reference or communication
    • the colors will be saved in a cookie and will return when you return
    • try these two (copy to signature field below): 
      {"backgroundA":"#8488d1","backgroundB":"#1c1c1c","upper":"#000000","middle":"#ff0000","lower":"#ffff00"}
{"backgroundA":"#ba85bf","backgroundB":"#2d4d2f","upper":"#ffff00","middle":"#2ec72e","lower":"#ff0000"}

    how to

    • define what colors are changeable in your css: 
      #DynamicColors_background {color: #ffefd5;}
    • use it anywhere in your css: 
      body {background-color: #ffefd5;}
    • init DynamicColors and pass in a div for the color controls: 
      $(function () {$.DynamicColors($("#my-colorpanel"));});
    • the color controls div will contain a list of color-pickers that allow you to dynamically adjust the colors on the page
    • changed colors are saved in a cookie, so they re-appear
    • you can copy the color settings from a text field to tell someone else to rate or enjoy it
